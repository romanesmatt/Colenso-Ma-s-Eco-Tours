Who We Are

Kia Ora! We at Colenso-Ma's Eco-Tours provide enviromentally sustainable tours in and around the greater Wellington Region. We will take you on a tour of everything that makes Wellington, Wellington. This includes the natural history of the region, organic food and beverage outlets, Maori history and culture and of course, Wellington's most significant attractions.

We operate carbon neutral transport like electric vehicles, although you have the option to walk on foot, or ride by bicycle or scooter. Just make a booking and we will see you on the ground in Wellington! Ka kite ano!

How We Strive For Sustainability

Colenso-Ma's Eco-Tours aims to be a carbon neutral tour company by 2024. We have made strides to ensure we meet this goal for the long term well-being of our planet.

Our fleet vehicles are all electric. Thanks to Nissan New Zealand, our fleet of Nissan EV's transport our customers and employees safe and sound while producing no harmful gases. We have also partnered with Flamingo Scooters and Onzo Bikes to provide electric scooters and bicycles respectively. You also have the option to walk on foot if you would like that extra bit of exercise during your tour!

Our main offices also operate in an enviromentally sustainable manner. We have policies onboard which restrict us to using energy efficient appliances, that we conserve energy and water where possible and most importantly to challenge ourselves to preserve the beautiful world we have.
